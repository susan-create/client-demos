Our Story

At Chaim's Pizza Shop, we believe in the art of pizza making. Established in 2008, our journey began with a simple passion: to bring authentic, high-quality pizza to our community. We meticulously select the freshest local ingredients, from sun-ripened tomatoes to premium mozzarella, ensuring every bite is a delightful experience.

What makes us special? It's our unwavering commitment to traditional recipes, combined with a creative flair that keeps our menu exciting. Our dough is hand-kneaded daily, slow-fermented for a perfect crust, and baked to perfection in our stone ovens. We cater to families, food enthusiasts, and anyone who appreciates a truly great slice.

Come and taste the difference that dedication and genuine love for pizza can make. We're more than just a pizza shop; we're a place where delicious memories are made.

Chaim's Signature Offering

The Chaim's Royale

Discover our culinary masterpiece, The Chaim's Royale. This exquisite pizza features a delicate truffle cream base, topped with succulent prosciutto, wild arugula, fresh burrata cheese, and a finishing drizzle of fig glaze. It's a symphony of flavors designed to elevate your pizza experience.

Created by Chaim himself, this signature dish embodies our commitment to innovation while honoring classic techniques. A true gourmet delight for those seeking something extraordinary.
